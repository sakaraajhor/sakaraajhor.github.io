Managing your website

This whole site is yours to update anytime — no code, no help needed. Here's everything in a few simple steps.

1 Signing in

Open your site and scroll to the very bottom — there's a small Admin link in the footer. Click it, then Continue with Google and sign in with your account. That's it — only you can get in.

2 Editing your words & details

From the dashboard, click Edit Content. You can change:

  • Your intro, the Profile paragraphs, and the three little fact boxes
  • Your contact details — email, phone, location, LinkedIn
  • The reference at the bottom

Edit any field and press Save changes. Your site updates within seconds.

3 Updating your CV

Still in Edit Content, find the Curriculum Vitae box at the top. Choose a new PDF and click Upload & publish. The download button on your site instantly points to the new file. Replace it as often as you like.

4 Adding research, sections & photos

In Edit Content, scroll down to manage your cards:

  • Experience, Research, Skills, and Leadership — add, edit, reorder (↑ ↓), or delete (✕) any entry
  • Custom sections — create entirely new sections with your own title and cards (great for publications, awards, anything)
  • Research and custom cards can hold an image — just click Upload on that card

Press Save changes when you're done.

5 Replying to messages

When someone messages you through the chat bubble on your site, it appears under Messages. A red dot shows unread chats. Click a conversation, type a reply, and send — they'll see it in real time.

6 Seeing your visitors

The Analytics tile shows how many people viewed your site, downloaded your CV, opened the chat, and clicked your links — with a graph of the last two weeks. Just for your eyes.

This site will grow with you.
Keep it yours. 💛
